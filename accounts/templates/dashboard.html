Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using netXwork

How do I find the right teammates for my project?

Use our Teammates Finder feature to search for potential team members based on skills, location, and project interests. You can filter results to find the perfect match for your project. Once you find someone interesting, send them a connection request with details about your project and what role you're looking to fill.

How do I create and promote an event?

To create an event, go to the Events section and click on "Create Event." Fill in all the required details about your event, including title, description, date, time, location (online or physical), and any registration links. Once submitted, your event will be reviewed by our moderators and published upon approval. You can promote your event by sharing it with your connections on netXwork or through social media.

Can I contribute learning resources to the platform?

Yes! We encourage community contributions to our Learning Zone. You can add courses, podcasts, articles, and other educational content by clicking "Add Course" or "Add Resource" buttons in the Learning Zone section. All submitted content goes through a moderation process to ensure quality and relevance before being published on the platform.

Is netXwork completely free to use?

netXwork offers a free basic membership that gives you access to most platform features. We also have premium plans with additional benefits like advanced search filters, unlimited connection requests, featured profile placement, and access to exclusive events and resources. You can view our pricing plans in the Account Settings section.
